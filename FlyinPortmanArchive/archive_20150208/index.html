Flyin Portman History!

The actual name of "Flyin Portman" was created after reading a very unique story about a mysterious ship that was said to be lost within the Bermuda Triangle. This ship was called Mary Celeste. This unique story seemed to fit our theme of being a well diverse organization with the mysterious concept along with multi cultures, platforms, and attributes. With this initially being who we were representing, we decided this was the direction we wanted to head! Together as members, together as an organization, but importantly together as a Prestige Gamer Community...the name Flyin Portman was born!

We establish this organization in 2012 with a group of strangers (Now Friends!) that decided to escape reality of the work life, parenting, and of course everyday stress events. We all had the exact same vision but so many different approaches of accomplishing this same vision. But of course, up until now the vision was only just that....Until Now!

Without hesitation we collaborated our independent visions & started to share and spread this within our family, friends, and even co-workers. This idea quickly grew and structure started to take place. We now represent a prestige community of gamers that “love” the online gaming experience no matter the platform, console, or time. You heard it correctly we have designed a system where it brings gamers from all over the world no matter the platform, console, or time!
