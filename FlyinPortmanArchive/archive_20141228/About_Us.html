Welcome to the Flyin Portman!

So before you start to assume who we are, we are not a larger scale guild or a clan! Call us what you want! We would like to tell you that we are unlike any other online gaming group. Our plan is to create a social networking connection with structure that allows friends to chat about games, platforms, new game launches, or events. More importantly our goal is to create a network of strangers coming together to make friends with one common cause, "Enjoy Online Gaming!" Our mission is to connect gamers worldwide no matter the time, game or platform you play into one central location. Flyin Portman Organization takes pride in developing pre-screened gamers to help match other with the same attributes. Making the distance between potential friends just one click away. We have created an organization that will link you with immediate friends for an extraordinaire online experience.

Imagine a system where we take profiling capability of an existing dating site (with the love part focusing on online gaming...of course) coming together with a professional social networking site but only for gamers. Gamers with like attributes, interests, or even like style. We are striving to make sure that everyone that is part of this organization feels welcomed and feels the level of respect and excitement of playing current & future gaming as a way to develop life long friendships.

Imagine buying a game you have waited over 6 months to launch and at your fingertips you have prescreened gamers waiting for your friend request. Imagine playing for 7+ hours with this individual and you have no issues sharing tips, tricks, strategies, or resources. Well gamers you have spoken and someone has heard. This group of strangers from 2012, now friends, bring to you their vision, their friends, more importantly their family...Flyin Portman. Browse our site learn who we are and request to join! We are looking for new members monthly.
