The first step to be considered for a potential member is to the complete the survey link below. To join our Flyin Portman Organization is absolutely FREE!  You will receive access to our complete House of Portable along with our in house gamer tags, chat, & reviews.  You will also have access to our merchandising purchasing program.

Donation of $10 denomination will give you full access to all Houses & Platforms. This access with provide all in house Gamer Tags, chats, reviews, & merchandising purchasing program. As we continue to grow as an organization, we will be offering other perks & benefits with other Tiered Donation Denominations.
